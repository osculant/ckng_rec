Cook Delicious Meals At Home

Discover easy step-by-step recipes you can cook at home using simple ingredients. Perfect for beginners and food lovers.

Classic Pancakes

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
Instructions:

Mix all ingredients into a smooth batter. Heat a pan on medium heat. Pour small batter portions and cook until bubbles form. Flip and cook until golden brown. Serve with honey or maple syrup.

Garlic Butter Pasta

Ingredients:
  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Salt & pepper
  • Grated parmesan cheese
Instructions:

Cook pasta according to package instructions. Melt butter in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant. Add cooked pasta and toss well. Season with salt, pepper, and top with parmesan cheese.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1 carrot sliced
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
Instructions:

Heat oil in a wok. Add vegetables and stir fry for 4–5 minutes. Add soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes. Serve hot with rice or noodles.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon juice
Instructions:

Toast bread slices. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Spread evenly on toast and serve immediately for a healthy breakfast.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili, garam masala)
Instructions:

Sauté onions until golden. Add ginger garlic paste and tomatoes. Add spices and chicken. Cook for 20 minutes until chicken is tender. Serve with rice or naan.

Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients:
  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp oil
Instructions:

Mix all ingredients in a mug until smooth. Microwave for 60–90 seconds. Let it cool slightly and enjoy a quick chocolate dessert.
